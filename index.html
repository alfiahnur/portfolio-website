Anyer

With my family, we went on holiday to Anyer Beach. Upon arrival, we rented a villa right on the beach. We enjoyed playing in the sand and swimming in the sea. In the evening, we enjoyed dinner on the beach while watching the sunset. Around the campfire, we shared stories together. The next morning, we enjoyed water activities before returning home with sweet memories of the beauty of Anyer Beach.

Pangandaran

This time I visited Pangandaran beach, this was not my first visit, and I visited again because I like the beach, the place is beautiful so there are lots of visitors from various cities, in Pangandaran there is a ship that was sunk by Mrs. Susi Pujiastuti, Minister of Pangandaran. Maritime Affairs and Fisheries of the Republic of Indonesia, and is now one of the places that visitors must pay attention to, not only that, there are also tourist attractions called white sand and shark rock. Pangandaran is the result of the expansion of Ciamis Regency, and was determined on October 25 2012 to become Pangandaran Regency.

Borobudur

This trip was to Borobudur temple, which is located in Magelang, Central Java, at that time the school held a study tour together, Borobudur temple was built between the 8th century, it took 75-100 years to complete during the reign of King Samaratungga, not to the temple Just Borobudur I also visited the smart park which is located in Yogyakarta.

Monas

My trip to Jakarta was really interesting! This city does have its own charm with all its glitter. From the bustling streets to the towering buildings, Jakarta is truly stunning. And Monas is certainly an unforgettable icon. With a height of 132 meters and a gold monument weighing 50 kilograms, Monas is a symbol of pride for the people of Jakarta and Indonesia as a whole. We hope your trip to Jakarta brings you many happy memories!
